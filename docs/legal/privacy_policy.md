# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2024

## Introduction

CrisisLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Organization details
- Profile preferences

**Content You Submit:**
- Crisis items and reports
- Claims and verifications
- Comments and annotations
- Photos and media

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data:**
- IP address (anonymized after 90 days)
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and actions taken
- Timestamps of activities

**Cookies and Tracking:**
- Session cookies (necessary)
- Analytics cookies (with consent)
- Preference cookies

### 3. Third-Party Data

**Public Sources:**
- Twitter/X posts (public API)
- RSS feeds (public)
- Reddit posts (public)
- News articles (public)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Process and verify crisis information
   - Provide real-time alerts
   - Enable collaboration features

2. **Analytics and Improvement**
   - Understand usage patterns
   - Improve platform performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Share important notifications

4. **Security and Compliance**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Enforce Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

1. **Consent** - For analytics and marketing
2. **Contract** - To provide our services
3. **Legitimate Interest** - For security and improvement
4. **Legal Obligation** - For compliance requirements

### We Share Data With:

**Legal Requirements:**

## Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

### You Have the Right To:

1. **Access** - Request copy of your data
2. **Rectification** - Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure** - Request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
4. **Portability** - Receive data in portable format
5. **Object** - Object to processing
6. **Restrict** - Limit how we use your data
7. **Withdraw Consent** - At any time

### How to Exercise Rights:

- **Data Export**: Account Settings → Export Data
- **Data Deletion**: Account Settings → Delete Account
- **Email**: privacy@crisislens.io
- **Response Time**: Within 30 days

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Active + 2 years |
| Content/items | Indefinite (anonymized after deletion) |
| Activity logs | 2 years |
| Audit logs | 7 years (compliance) |
| Backups | 90 days |

## Data Security

### Security Measures:

- **Encryption**: TLS/SSL in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens, 2FA available
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring
- **Auditing**: Immutable audit trails
- **Backups**: Daily encrypted backups

### In Case of Breach:

- Notification within 72 hours (GDPR)
- Email to affected users
- Report to authorities as required
